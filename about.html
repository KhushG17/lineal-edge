About Lineal Edge

Who we are?

Legacy Personal Finance Solutions

Lineal Edge Private Limited is built on over five decades of financial stewardship, guiding families across generations with discipline, clarity, and trust. What began with instilling the fundamentals of saving has evolved into a comprehensive approach to personal finance.

Today, we combine generational wisdom with modern financial strategies to help individuals and families preserve, protect, and grow their wealth with confidence. Our focus is not just on managing portfolios, but on building lasting financial legacies that stand the test of time.

Our Mission

To help individuals and families preserve, protect, and grow their wealth through disciplined, personalized financial strategies combining generational wisdom with modern financial solutions.

Our Vision

To redefine wealth management as a generational journey, built on trust, clarity, and long-term thinking.

Our Journey
Chapter 01 1970

Planting the Seed

In 1970, Late Shri Ram Chand Mutreja laid the very first roots of the family legacy by instilling the discipline of saving, trust, and financial responsibility into households across generations. His work planted the original sapling on which Lineal Edge stands today.

Chapter 02 1988

Nurturing & Protecting the Roots

In 1988, Mukesh Kumar Mutreja carried that foundation forward with discipline and care, strengthening client relationships and expanding the practice into structured protection and long-term wealth building. This was the stage where the sapling matured into a rooted, resilient tree.

Chapter 03 2023

The Canopy & The Harvest

In 2023, Divy Mutreja began shaping the next phase of Lineal Edge by bringing a modern lens to generational wealth. With deeper strategy, broader solutions, and a sharper long-term vision, the tree now enters a fuller stage of growth marked by abundance, confidence, and lasting legacy.
