About Lineal Edge

Who we are?

Legacy Personal Finance Solutions

Lineal Edge Private Limited is built on over five decades of financial stewardship, guiding families across generations with discipline, clarity, and trust. What began with instilling the fundamentals of saving has evolved into a comprehensive approach to personal finance.

Today, we combine generational wisdom with modern financial strategies to help individuals and families preserve, protect, and grow their wealth with confidence. Our focus is not just on managing portfolios, but on building lasting financial legacies that stand the test of time.

Our Mission

To help individuals and families preserve, protect, and grow their wealth through disciplined, personalized financial strategies combining generational wisdom with modern financial solutions.

Our Vision

To redefine wealth management as a generational journey, built on trust, clarity, and long-term thinking.

Our Journey
Chapter 01 1971

Planting the Seed

Over five decades ago, Name began traveling 50 kilometers a day on a bicycle to instill the raw discipline of savings into families. By distributing home piggy banks to children, he planted the very first seeds of financial responsibility. He laid the deep, structural groundwork that true wealth requires.

Chapter 02 1990

Nurturing & Protecting the Roots

As the seedling emerged, Name stepped in to nurture and defend it. For decades, our focus was on shielding our clients from external threats through comprehensive life and health insurance, ensuring the roots grew strong and unbreakable. As the markets evolved, we guided that protected capital into Mutual Funds, watering the roots through the power of compounding.

Chapter 03 2023

The Canopy & The Harvest

Today, that carefully protected sapling has grown into a lush, solid tree. With the entry of Name, Lineal Edge is focused on the harvest. We honor the deep roots of our existing clients while welcoming new families by offering more complex, best-in-class, high-yield "fruits" - such as Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs), Portfolio Management Services (PMS), and comprehensive Estate Planning.
