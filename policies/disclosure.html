4. Regular Plans vs. Direct Plans

Lineal Edge Private Limited deals in Regular Plans only for Mutual Fund Schemes, from which we earn a trailing commission on client investments. Complete disclosure of these commission earnings is provided to clients prior to investment.

Please be aware that a Direct Plan option is available for every Mutual Fund Scheme, which offers the advantage of a lower expense ratio. Because we are not entitled to earn commissions on Direct Plans, we do not distribute or deal in them.
