Our Journey
Chapter 01 1971

Planting the Seed

Over five decades ago, Name began traveling 50 kilometers a day on a bicycle to instill the raw discipline of savings into families. By distributing home piggy banks to children, he planted the very first seeds of financial responsibility. He laid the deep, structural groundwork that true wealth requires.

Chapter 02 1990

Nurturing & Protecting the Roots

As the seedling emerged, Name stepped in to nurture and defend it. For decades, our focus was on shielding our clients from external threats through comprehensive life and health insurance, ensuring the roots grew strong and unbreakable. As the markets evolved, we guided that protected capital into Mutual Funds, watering the roots through the power of compounding.

Chapter 03 2023

The Canopy & The Harvest

Today, that carefully protected sapling has grown into a lush, solid tree. With the entry of Name, Lineal Edge is focused on the harvest. We honor the deep roots of our existing clients while welcoming new families by offering more complex, best-in-class, high-yield "fruits" — such as Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs), Portfolio Management Services (PMS), and comprehensive Estate Planning. We pride ourselves on delivering these sophisticated, high-value solutions with brilliant technical and real-world clarity.
